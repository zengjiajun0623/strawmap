How to read this draft

This lab is not an Ethereum mainnet upgrade. It keeps the original strawmap's CL, DL, EL layer model and north-star framing, then turns Vitalik's July 4, 2026 Lean Ethereum themes into small executable artifacts that can grow into a serious research/devnet program.
